Ptuj

Ptuj is a town in northeastern Slovenia. It is the oldest recorded city in Slovenia, it has been inhabited since the late Stone Age.
Very special about the town is it's traditional charm underlined by it's old buildings full of history like ptuj grad (the castle of ptuj) and ptujska klet vinarstvo (Ptuj wine cellar). Also, being located next to the drava (drau) and having many beautiful gardens and parks, it is perfect for every one wanting to enjoy a great city and the wonder of nature with a fascinating history.
